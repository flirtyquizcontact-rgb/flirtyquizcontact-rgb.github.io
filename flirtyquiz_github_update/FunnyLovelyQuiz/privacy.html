Privacy Policy

App: Funny Lovely Quiz
Developer: Flirty Quiz Studio
Effective date: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Funny Lovely Quiz handles information when you use the Android app.

1. Entertainment-only app

Funny Lovely Quiz is a light entertainment quiz app. Quiz results, compatibility-style messages, romantic prompts and humorous outcomes are for fun only and should not be treated as relationship advice, psychological advice, medical advice or professional guidance.

2. Information we may process

Funny Lovely Quiz may process limited app-related information needed to operate, improve and protect the app, including:

  • quiz answers, selected choices, score/result state, app settings and preferences;
  • names or short text entered into quiz fields, if the app version includes these fields;
  • device and app information such as app version, device model, operating system version and performance data;
  • crash logs and diagnostic information used to identify and fix bugs;
  • advertising identifiers, ad interaction information and consent signals where ads are shown;
  • purchase or entitlement state if the app offers premium features or in-app purchases through Google Play.

3. Local data

Quiz choices, saved results, preferences and similar app data may be stored locally on your device. Do not enter sensitive personal information into quiz fields.

4. Third-party services

The app may use third-party services depending on the current app version, including:

  • Google AdMob and Google User Messaging Platform for ads and consent management;
  • Firebase Analytics for app analytics and usage measurement;
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting and diagnostics;
  • Google Play Billing for purchases, premium unlocks and entitlement restoration.

These services process data according to their own policies and systems.

5. Advertising

The app may display ads. Advertising partners may process device identifiers, ad request information, ad interaction data and consent signals to provide, limit, measure or personalize ads where permitted by law and your consent choices.

6. Purchases and premium features

If the app includes premium features or in-app purchases, transactions are processed by Google Play. We do not directly process your payment card details.

7. Children

The app is intended for entertainment audiences appropriate to its Google Play content rating. If you are under the minimum legal age in your country, use the app only with permission from a parent or legal guardian.

8. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited information may be shared with third-party service providers only as needed for ads, diagnostics, analytics, purchases, fraud prevention, security, legal compliance and app operation.

9. Data retention and deletion

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it, clear app data or uninstall the app. Data processed by third-party services is retained according to their own policies. The app does not require an account created with us, so there is no developer-hosted account deletion flow.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate for the app. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

11. Your choices

  • You can manage ad consent where a consent screen is available.
  • You can manage Android permissions from device settings when applicable.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: flirtyquiz.contact@gmail.com.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
