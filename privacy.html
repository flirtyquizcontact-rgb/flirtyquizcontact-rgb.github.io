Privacy Policy

App: File Transfer: Ultimatum Share (UltimatumFlashShare)
Effective date: 2026-03-11
Support email: flirtyquiz.contact@gmail.com

Summary: File Transfer: Ultimatum Share is an offline-first local file transfer and file tools app. File transfers are designed to happen locally between devices on local networks or through user-initiated local sharing flows. We do not operate our own cloud backend for file transfers and we do not upload your selected files to our own servers for transfer. The App may use third-party Google services such as Firebase, Google Mobile Ads (AdMob), and Google Play Billing for diagnostics, analytics, advertising, purchase handling, and app sustainability.

1) Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how File Transfer: Ultimatum Share (“App”, “we”, “us”, “our”) accesses, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the App.

2) Information we collect

2.1 Data you choose to provide or access

  • Files you select: The App accesses files only when you explicitly choose them through Android system mechanisms such as MediaStore, the Storage Access Framework (SAF), document pickers, or folder pickers.
  • Local file transfer content: Files chosen by you may be copied, received, previewed, exported, or stored locally on your device or transferred locally to another device that you intentionally connect to.
  • Support communications: If you contact us, you may voluntarily provide your email address and the contents of your message.

2.2 Information processed locally on your device

  • App settings and preferences: The App may store preferences locally on your device, such as theme choices, selected folders, selected roots, app options, premium entitlement state, and local feature settings.
  • Locally saved files: Received files, exported files, app-private files, Vault files, bridge files, and other user-initiated local content may be stored in app-specific device storage until you delete them or export them elsewhere.
  • Local network session data: For features such as LAN transfer, QR pairing, PC Connect, Smart Sync, Folder Sync, or Folder Browser Pro, the App may process local IP addresses, local ports, session tokens, selected roots, device discovery data, and local file metadata on-device and on your local network.

2.3 Data processed by third-party services

The App may use third-party services provided by Google. These services may process information according to their own policies and your device/account settings.

  • Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics: crash reports, analytics data, performance information, app events, device/app information, and related technical diagnostics used to improve stability, analyze usage, troubleshoot issues, monitor app quality, and support app integrity-related review.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): advertising-related data such as ad requests, ad impressions, interactions, device or app identifiers, IP address, diagnostics, and fraud/abuse prevention signals, subject to Google’s SDK behavior, your consent settings, and applicable law.
  • Google Play Billing: purchase state, entitlement validation, purchase tokens, order-related purchase signals, and restore-purchase information required to unlock and manage Premium features purchased through Google Play.

3) How we use information

  • Provide offline/local file transfer features.
  • Enable QR pairing, LAN discovery, PC Connect, Smart Sync, Folder Sync, Folder Browser Pro, Vault workflows, and other user-initiated local tools.
  • Store your app preferences and premium state locally on your device.
  • Support one-time Premium unlocks and purchase restoration through Google Play Billing.
  • Display advertising in the free version of the App, where applicable.
  • Improve app stability, analytics, security-related review, debugging, and performance through diagnostics.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, unauthorized use, and app integrity issues.

4) Advertising and Premium

  • Free version: The App may show ads, including interstitial ads, in certain parts of the free experience.
  • Premium version: Premium may remove ads and unlock additional features such as PC Connect Pro, Transfer Resume, Secure Transfer, Multi-Channel, Folder Sync, Folder Browser Pro, and related advanced functionality depending on the version of the App and the entitlement state stored on your device.
  • Purchases: Premium purchases and restore operations are processed via Google Play Billing, not by us directly.

5) Permissions used

The App requests access only for features you choose to use. Access is intended to be user-initiated.

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES – to let you browse, select, preview, transfer, or manage images.
  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO – to let you browse, select, preview, transfer, or manage videos.
  • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO – to let you browse, select, preview, transfer, or manage audio files.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (older Android versions only) – legacy compatibility for file access on supported devices.
  • Storage Access Framework / document and folder pickers – to let you explicitly choose files or folders, including downloads, documents, and user-selected folder roots.
  • NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES – for nearby/local device discovery where required by Android.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION (legacy Android compatibility only) – for LAN discovery compatibility on Android versions where the OS requires location-related permission for nearby/network discovery.
  • Network access – for local transfer, QR/LAN connection features, diagnostics, ads, and purchase validation where applicable.

No cloud transfer backend operated by us. No sale of your personal data by us.

6) Data sharing

We do not sell your files or personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Google service providers such as Firebase, AdMob, and Google Play Billing, as needed for diagnostics, analytics, advertising, fraud prevention, purchases, and entitlement management.
  • Other devices you intentionally connect to during local transfer, PC Connect, Smart Sync, Folder Sync, or Folder Browser sessions on your local network.
  • Authorities or regulators if required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable government request.

7) Your files and local storage

  • Your ownership: You retain ownership of the files you choose to access, transfer, store, or export through the App.
  • Local handling: Files selected for transfer are intended to remain local unless you explicitly share or export them using another app or service.
  • Received content: Received files may be stored in app-specific or user-selected storage locations on your device.
  • Vault: Vault files may be stored locally in app-controlled storage and protected by the security mechanisms implemented by the current App version.
  • Folder Browser Pro / Smart Sync / PC Connect: user-selected folder roots, file manifests, and session metadata may be processed locally to support those features.

8) Legal bases (EEA/UK)
