Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Block Buster Vortex handles information when you use the Android game.

1. App Information

  • App name: Block Buster Vortex
  • Package name: com.blockbustervortex
  • Platform: Android / Google Play

2. Information We May Process

Block Buster Vortex may process limited app-related data needed to operate, improve and protect the game, including:

  • game progress, scores, achievements, rewards, settings and cosmetic selections;
  • device and app information such as app version, device model, operating system version and performance data;

  • advertising and consent-related information when ads are shown;

The app may use third-party services that process data according to their own policies, including:

The app may display ads. Advertising partners may use device identifiers, ad interaction data and consent signals to provide, limit, measure or personalize ads where permitted by law and your consent choices.

5. Google Play Games

If you choose to connect with Google Play Games, the app may access your Play Games display name, profile icon and player identifier to show your profile and submit achievements or leaderboard scores. You can manage Play Games account settings through Google services.

6. Local Game Data

Some gameplay data, preferences and cosmetic selections may be stored locally on your device. Removing the app or clearing app data may delete local progress unless a platform feature preserves it.

7. Children

The app is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent. If you are under the minimum legal age in your country, use the app only with permission from a parent or legal guardian.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect app-related information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to connect Google Play Games.
  • You can manage ad consent where the consent screen is available.
  • You can disable app permissions from Android system settings when applicable.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact email listed on the Google Play Store page for Block Buster Vortex.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
